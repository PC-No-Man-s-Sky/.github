## About No Man's Sky

No Man's Sky is a space exploration and survival game set in a vast procedurally generated universe. Players travel between star systems, discover unique planets, gather resources, and upgrade equipment while exploring an almost limitless galaxy.

The game combines exploration, crafting, base building, and survival systems. Players can land on planets, scan environments, mine materials, manage life support, and expand their capabilities through technology upgrades and improved gear.

With continuous updates and multiplayer features, players can explore together, build shared bases, and participate in large-scale events. The experience focuses on freedom, discovery, and long-term progression across a constantly evolving universe.
